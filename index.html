Services

Need help with a complex website or a simple landing page? I'm here for you! From design to launch, I'll guide you every step of the way. Let's turn your vision into a reality and get your website up and running in no time.

01 Consultation

Whether you're facing challenges with your current website or you want to build a new one, I am here to assist you. Let's have a conversation about your ideas and goals regarding your website. After understanding your needs, I can provide some advice on how to move forward.

02 Design & Content

I design stunning, user-friendly websites tailored to your goals. I also offer a content creation to get your site started, from crafting clear website copy to choosing captivating images. Let's turn your online vision into reality!

03 Website Build & Launch

Once we agree on your design, I will start building your awesome website! I code websites from scratch or work with popular platforms like WordPress, Wix, and Webflow. I can customize templates and/or work with custom code to create the website you envision.

jour
ney

My true strength lies in creating websites that are not just functional, but visually captivating and elegant.
After all, good design matters.

My coding journey ignited during high school when I wrote my first line of code. From there, it was a deep dive – months spent devouring online resources about frontend development. Building my first website from scratch solidified my path as a coder.

7 years ago, I was offered my first job as a front-end developer at TopMonks, a creative tech company based in Prague. I collaborated on a complete website redesign for them, along with coding projects for various Czech businesses, driving innovation and optimization.

Apart from that, I worked with companies like AIKIT Digital, Kindred, Refresh, or Byzkids to elevate the online presence of early-stage startups and growing businesses. My expertise lies in crafting beautiful, user-centric websites using HTML, CSS (including SCSS/SASS), JavaScript, and React.js. I'm equally skilled with web creation platforms like WordPress, Wix, and Webflow.
