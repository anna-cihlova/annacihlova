Journey

My true strength lies in creating experiences that are not just functional, but visually captivating and elegant.

Hello, I’m Anna, a frontend engineer specializing in building powerful, interactive websites and applications. With over 8 years of experience, I’ve developed expertise in JavaScript, TypeScript, React.js, Next.js, HTML, CSS/SCSS, and more! I’m also expanding my skills into AI engineering, bringing curiosity and innovation into every project I take on.

My journey started back in high school, when I wrote my first line of code and spent months diving deep into online resources. Building my first website from scratch confirmed that coding was the path for me. Since then, I’ve worked with companies like Publicis Groupe , Talent.com , TopMonks , AIKIT Digital , Byzkids , and Refresh . Across these roles, I’ve contributed to website redesigns, optimized user experiences, and helped elevate the online presence of startups and established businesses alike.

I’m a self-taught engineer who truly loves this work. Driven by curiosity and creativity, I’m always learning, improving, and challenging myself to be better. I bring a positive, forward-thinking mindset to every project, thriving both when collaborating across departments and when diving deep into independent work. I love to inspire and be inspired, always looking for ways to grow and help others do the same.
